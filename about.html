Who I Am

The engineer
behind the work.

I'm a frontend engineer with 6+ years of experience shipping web applications at scale — from scrappy early-stage startups to product teams at mid-size tech companies with millions of users.

My work lives at the intersection of engineering rigor and design sensitivity. I care deeply about the invisible things: the 60fps animation, the sub-100ms interaction, the layout that just feels right on first load.

I've led design systems, mentored junior engineers, driven accessibility audits, and owned core product surfaces end-to-end. When I'm not building, I contribute to open source and write about CSS architecture on my blog.

Originally from Manila, currently based in San Francisco. Always happy to grab a coffee and talk product, code, or design.

Core Values

01
Ship with intention.
Every decision — from component API to animation duration — has a reason behind it. No magic numbers, no mystery behavior.
02
Performance is a feature.
Fast products win. I treat LCP, CLS, and TTI as first-class product metrics, not post-launch checklist items.
03
Accessibility, always.
Good interfaces work for everyone. ARIA, keyboard navigation, and screen reader support aren't optional extras.
04
Collaborate relentlessly.
The best products are built when design, engineering, and product are genuinely aligned. I operate in that gap.
